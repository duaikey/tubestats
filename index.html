❓ Frequently Asked Questions
What is TubeStats?

TubeStats is a free YouTube analytics platform that lets you track real-time subscriber counts, views, estimated revenue, daily stats history, and growth projections for any YouTube channel — completely free, no sign-up needed.

How do I search for a channel?

Type the channel name or @handle (e.g. @MrBeast) in the search bar and press Enter or click Search. You can search from the homepage or the header search bar.

How accurate is the estimated revenue?

Revenue estimates are based on industry-average CPM rates of $2–$8 per 1,000 views. Actual earnings vary significantly depending on the channel's niche, audience country, ad types, and YouTube's policies. The estimates are for reference only.

What is the SB Grade?

The SB (Social Blade) Grade rates a channel's size from C to A++ based on subscriber count. A++ is reserved for channels with 50M+ subscribers like MrBeast and T-Series.

What is the Live Subscriber Count?

The live counter starts from the real subscriber count fetched from YouTube's API and simulates real-time growth based on the channel's average growth rate. It's an estimate — not an exact real-time feed.

Why is the Daily Statistics table empty?

Daily stats are saved the first time a channel is searched. Come back the next day after searching a channel and the daily history will start building up automatically. The more days pass, the more history you'll see.

How does the Compare feature work?

Click Compare in the navigation, enter two channel names or @handles, and click Search. TubeStats will fetch both channels and show a side-by-side comparison of subscribers, views, videos, and estimated revenue — with the winner highlighted in green.

How does Top Charts work?

Top Charts shows a ranked leaderboard of popular YouTube channels across categories like Global, Music, Gaming, and My Country (auto-detected by your location). Channels are ranked by subscriber count.

Is TubeStats affiliated with YouTube or Google?

No. TubeStats is an independent analytics tool powered by the YouTube Data API v3. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to YouTube or Google in any way.

Is TubeStats free?

Yes — 100% free, forever. No premium plans, no paywalls, no ads. All features are available to everyone at no cost.
